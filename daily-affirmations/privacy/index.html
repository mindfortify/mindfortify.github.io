Privacy Policy

App: Daily Affirmations. Developer: Mind Fortify. Last updated: 2026-05-21.

Contact

For privacy questions, account deletion requests, or data requests, email info@mindfortify.com.

Data Collected

  • Account data: email address, Firebase user ID, and email verification status.
  • Affirmation set data: set name, affirmation text, public/private status, owner user ID, and sync state for Firebase-backed sets.
  • Local app data: settings, selected theme, locally authored sets, and editor drafts stored on the device.
  • Technical data: Firebase, Crashlytics, and Performance may process app diagnostics and performance information depending on enabled build/runtime configuration.

How Data Is Used

  • To sign users in and verify email addresses.
  • To sync verified users' private Firebase-backed affirmation sets.
  • To display public affirmation sets to other users when a user chooses to publish a set.
  • To keep local-only sets and settings available on the device.
  • To diagnose crashes and performance issues when Firebase diagnostics are enabled.

Sharing

Published affirmation sets are visible to other users. Private Firebase-backed sets and local-only sets are not intentionally shared publicly.

The app uses Firebase services as backend infrastructure. Data processed by Firebase is handled under Google/Firebase service terms and controls.

Retention And Deletion

Local-only sets can be deleted in the app. Firebase-backed private sets can be deleted in the app. Editor drafts are cleared after successful save.

To request account deletion or help with data deletion, email info@mindfortify.com from the email address associated with your account.

Security

The app uses Firebase authentication and Firebase service security controls.

Wellbeing disclaimer: Daily Affirmations is a wellbeing and reflection tool. It is not medical advice, therapy, diagnosis, treatment, crisis support, or a substitute for a qualified professional.
