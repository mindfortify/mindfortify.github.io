Support

For help with Daily Affirmations, email info@mindfortify.com.

What To Send

  • For account deletion requests, email from the address associated with your account and include "Account deletion request" in the subject.
  • For public affirmation set reports, include the set name and why you are reporting it.
  • For privacy questions, include "Privacy request" in the subject.
  • Do not email passwords, service credentials, private keys, or other sensitive secrets.

Public Content Reports

Public affirmation sets can also be reported inside the app from the set selection screen. Reported sets may be unpublished, deleted, or restricted after review.

Daily Affirmations is not crisis support. If you may harm yourself or someone else, or if you feel unsafe, contact local emergency services or a trusted qualified professional immediately.
