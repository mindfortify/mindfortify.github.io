Terms And Safety

App: Daily Affirmations. Developer: Mind Fortify. Last updated: 2026-05-21.

Wellbeing Disclaimer

Daily Affirmations is a wellbeing and reflection tool. It is not medical advice, therapy, diagnosis, treatment, crisis support, or a substitute for a qualified professional.

If you may harm yourself or someone else, or if you feel unsafe, contact local emergency services or a trusted qualified professional immediately.

Acceptable Use

Users may create affirmation sets for personal use. Verified users may publish sets publicly if they are comfortable sharing that content with other users.

Users must not publish content that:

  • Harasses, threatens, shames, or targets another person or group.
  • Encourages self-harm, suicide, eating disorder behavior, violence, or dangerous activity.
  • Contains sexual content, exploitative content, hate, abuse, or bullying.
  • Misleads users into treating the app as medical or crisis support.
  • Contains personal information that should not be public.
  • Violates intellectual property rights or applicable law.

Moderation

Public affirmation sets can be reported from the set selection screen in the app. Reports are reviewed by Mind Fortify or an appointed moderator.

Reported public sets may be unpublished, deleted, or restricted. Repeat or serious misuse may lead to account restrictions.

For public content policy questions or moderation requests, email info@mindfortify.com.

Account And Verification

Private Firebase-backed set authoring requires a verified email address. Logged-out users can use default/public sets and local-only sets.
