Daily Affirmations

A Quiet Affirmation Practice

Choose from built-in and public affirmation sets, create local sets on your Android device, or sign in with a verified email address to back up private sets. Sessions are quiet, gesture-driven, and designed for reflection rather than gamification.

Features

  • Built-in affirmation content.
  • Public affirmation sets available to everyone.
  • Local custom sets saved on your device.
  • Verified-account private sets backed up with Firebase.
  • Optional publishing of user-created affirmation sets.
  • Offline-friendly behavior through bundled content and local caches.
  • Curated visual themes, reduced motion, and high-contrast readability options.

Wellbeing disclaimer: Daily Affirmations is a wellbeing and reflection tool. It is not medical advice, therapy, diagnosis, treatment, crisis support, or a substitute for a qualified professional.
